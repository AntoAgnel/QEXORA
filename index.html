Features

Everything you need for
outcome-based assessment

QEXORA automates the entire examination preparation workflow — from question storage to final PDF export — in one unified platform.

🧠
AI-Powered Mapping Suggestions
Powered by Groq's free Llama 3.3 70B model to intelligently suggest Bloom's Taxonomy Level, Knowledge Category, Course Outcome, and PI codes — with full reasoning for each decision.
⚡
Smart Question Selection
Selects questions from your bank ensuring balanced difficulty distribution, complete CO coverage, and proper cognitive skill evaluation based on Bloom's taxonomy.
✏️
Visual Paper Editor
Drag-and-drop block editor to customise every element of your question paper — text, images, sections, dividers — before exporting as PDF.
📊
CO Attainment Analytics
Automatically generates course outcome coverage reports, Bloom's level distribution charts, and difficulty analytics for every generated paper.
📄
Professional PDF Export
Exports clean, print-ready A4 PDF question papers with full academic mapping tags, section headers, and outcome summary tables.
🔐
Secure & Role-Based
Faculty and Admin roles with Google OAuth sign-in, OTP-based password reset via email, 30-minute session timeout, and a dedicated developer panel.
Workflow

From question to paper in minutes

A streamlined 5-step process that handles mapping, selection, generation, and export automatically.

1
Select Institution
Choose Engineering, Arts & Science, or School to activate the correct academic mapping framework automatically.
2
Add Questions
Add questions to your centralised bank with auto-suggested Bloom's level, Knowledge Category, CO, and PI code.
3
Pick a Template
Choose from ready-made templates or create a custom one defining sections, marks, and difficulty ratios.
4
Generate Paper
One click generates a balanced, outcome-mapped question paper with complete analytics and CO attainment report.
5
Edit & Export
Open in the visual editor to customise layout and content, then export as a professional A4 PDF or print directly.
